A quick presentation of the franchise

In Animal Crossing , the player character is a human who lives in a village inhabited by various anthropomorphic animals, carrying out various activities such as fishing, bug catching, and fossil hunting. The series is notable for its open-ended gameplay and extensive use of the video game console's internal clock and calendar to simulate real passage of time.

Animal Crossing was first launched in 2001 on the Nintendo 64. In New Horizons, the latest version of the franchise, the story starts with the player living on a deserted island as part of the Nook Inc. Getaway Package. The new version introduces new features and mechanics that make the game more enjoyable than ever. In addition, the game's design has been enhanced and its aesthetics are now more detailed than ever.
